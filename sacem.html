What I Did

Key contributions and design decisions made throughout this project.

  • User Research & Interviews — Conducted in-depth interviews with SACEM members to map pain points in the existing messaging flow and identify unmet needs.
  • Information Architecture — Restructured the message hierarchy and notification system to reduce cognitive load and surface the most relevant content first.
  • UI Redesign — Designed a modern, accessible messaging interface aligned with SACEM's design system, introducing threading, rich-text replies, and attachment previews.
  • Prototyping & Testing — Built interactive Figma prototypes and ran two rounds of usability testing with real users, iterating on findings between sessions.
  • Design Handoff — Delivered developer-ready specs, annotated edge cases, and supported the engineering team during implementation to ensure pixel-accurate delivery.
