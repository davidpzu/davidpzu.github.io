Over the years I’ve grown from a fresh junior designer into a professional with solid experience in UI/UX and working with clients. I have had the chance of working closely with clients which has taught me how to understand their needs, translating them into modern solutions, as well as deliver projects that answer user needs. As a result these experiences have changed the way I approach design: as a tool to solve real problems and create meaningful user experiences.

Beyond design, I’ve discovered a passion for languages and music, which have opened doors to incredible experiences across Europe and even to China, where I lived for half a year. For the past eight years I’ve been based in Madrid, a city that I have felt in love with for it's international atmosphere and the opportunities it offers to keep learning, growing, and getting to know experts in the design industry, whether it be events or hangouts in a collaborative space.
